    Intent & Goal

    StudyIQ is an all-in-one university portal designed to connect students, teachers, and parents in a single platform. It solves the problem of fragmented academic information by centralizing grades, assignments, attendance, schedules, announcements, messaging, GPA tracking, and course management. The goal is to give every role clear visibility and control over academic life, improving outcomes and communication across the university.

    Audience & Roles

    Three roles use the platform: (1) Students — view grades, assignments, attendance, schedule, GPA, enroll in courses, receive announcements, and message teachers; (2) Teachers — post grades, create and manage assignments, record attendance, post announcements, manage their courses, and message students/parents; (3) Parents — monitor their child's grades, attendance, GPA, and receive announcements and messages from teachers. An Admin role manages user accounts, course catalog, and school-wide settings.

    Core Flows

    These flows must work end-to-end:

    Student signs up / logs in → lands on personalized dashboard showing GPA, upcoming assignments, today's schedule, and recent announcements

    Teacher creates a course → adds students to the course → posts assignments with due dates → grades submissions → attendance is recorded per class session

    Student views their courses → opens an assignment → submits work → views grade and teacher feedback

    Teacher posts a grade or marks attendance → parent receives an update and can view it in their dashboard

    Student views full semester schedule and course timetable → clicks a course to see details, assignments, and grades

    Student views GPA tracker showing cumulative GPA, GPA per semester, and grade breakdown by course

    Teacher or Admin posts a school-wide or class-specific announcement → students and parents see it in their feed

    Teacher sends a message to a student or parent → recipient replies → full conversation thread is visible to both parties

    Admin creates user accounts (students, teachers, parents) → links parents to their children → assigns teachers to courses

    Student browses course catalog → enrolls in available courses for the semester → enrollment reflected in their schedule and dashboard

    Technical Requirements

    Role-based access control for Student, Teacher, Parent, and Admin. Each role sees a different dashboard and navigation. Entities needed: Users (with role), Courses, Assignments, Submissions, Grades, Attendance Records, Announcements, Messages, Enrollments, and GPA snapshots. GPA is calculated automatically from grade records. Parent-child linking for parent accounts.

    Design Preferences

    Clean and professional aesthetic. Light background (white/off-white), navy blue or deep blue primary accents (#1E3A5F or similar), structured card-based layout, clear typographic hierarchy with a modern sans-serif font. Sidebar navigation for role-based sections. Feels trustworthy, academic, and easy to scan. Subtle use of color to indicate status (green for passing, red for at-risk, amber for pending). Mobile-friendly and responsive.
